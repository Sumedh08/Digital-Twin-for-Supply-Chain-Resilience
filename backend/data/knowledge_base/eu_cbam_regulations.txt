=== EU CARBON BORDER ADJUSTMENT MECHANISM (CBAM) ===
Regulation (EU) 2023/956 of the European Parliament

ARTICLE 1 - SUBJECT MATTER
This Regulation establishes a carbon border adjustment mechanism (CBAM) for addressing greenhouse gas emissions embedded in the goods listed in Annex I upon their importation into the customs territory of the Union.

ARTICLE 2 - SCOPE
Applies to goods listed in Annex I:
- Iron and Steel: CN 7206-7229
- Aluminium: CN 7601-7616 
- Cement: CN 2523
- Fertilisers: CN 2808, 2814, 3102-3105
- Electricity: CN 2716
- Hydrogen: CN 2804

ARTICLE 6 - CBAM CERTIFICATE OBLIGATIONS
Each authorized CBAM declarant shall, by 31 May of each year, surrender a number of CBAM certificates corresponding to the embedded emissions in goods imported during the preceding calendar year.

TRANSITIONAL PERIOD (Oct 2023 - Dec 2025):
- Importers must report embedded emissions quarterly.
- No financial obligation during this period.
- Default values may be used for up to 100% of emissions.

DEFINITIVE PERIOD (from Jan 2026):
- Financial obligations commence. CBAM certificates must be purchased.

EXEMPTIONS (Article 2):
- Goods originating in Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway, Switzerland.
- Goods with total value below EUR 150 in a consignment.
